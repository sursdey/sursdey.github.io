Our Services

Yoga Class for Employees

Offering yoga for staff members can have a wonderful effect on them, helping to alleviate stress and enable them to manage their jobs with more ease. Classes can be designed to suit their needs and include a gentle and sensitive physical practice along with restorative poses.

Sreypov

Founder & Coach

Sreypov has been practicing at Krama yoga for over ten years. She achieved her first teacher training with Karma yoga called “Bodysmart life skills training” in 2014 the program is a 2 years program. The teacher training includes anatomy philosophy, asana practice, teaching methodology, Kids yoga, and philosophy. After graduation, she started to teach kids with the NGO partner in Phnom Penh and the province. In 2015, she continued her journey with Azahar Foundation to continue her education. Sreypov has joined a program called “Peace Program “ that is organized by the Azahar foundation. That program includes “ Yoga practice, Leadership, Theater, Non-violence communication, and some other workshops to strengthen as a preparation for another training. In 2017, she finished her 200hr teacher training that certifies by the UK Alliance.

While training, She also did a small program as a part of the training is Fly yoga which some might call” Aerial yoga “. She is currently finishing a degree in International Relations and the Royal University of Law and Economics, Graduated from Ambassador Youth Council at the US Embassy. Vat Sreypov has appointed as an NGO Manager at Krama yoga Cambodia in mid-2020.

Last year she worked for the Embassy Of India, Phnom Penh as a yoga teacher and cultural assistant to the Ambassador. Currently, she has initiated her community project called “ Sursdey yoga community outreach project”.

Thyna

Yoga Teacher

Thyna is a yoga teacher at AZAHAR Cambodia and holds both a 200-hour certification and Fly Fit yoga certification from the UK Yoga Alliance recognized “Yoga Soul Body Fit”. The AZAHAR Foundation has sponsored her since 2017. She is now pursuing a 300-hour certified Jivamukti Yoga Teacher Training. She has learned different kinds of yoga such as Hatha Yoga and Ashtanga vinyasa as well as attending AZAHAR’s 2-year Peace Program. Her classes focus on breathing with movement flow from one pose to another, as well as workouts and conditioning to improve strength. She incorporates meditation and relaxation in order to calm one’s mind and soul. Thyna also chants some mantras in Sanskrit language with the harmonium (traditional Indian instrument). As well as a dedicated yoga practitioner, she’s also a Private Khmer language teacher for both Cambodian and foreign students. Currently, she is employed by the UK-based “What3words” company as a Khmer Language Consultant.

Phary

Yoga Teacher

Through Phary’s contagious passion for yoga and 11 years of practice in the field, 6 years of which she spent teaching kids and adults, Phary provides Gentle Yoga classes and private sessions that are a fusion of playfulness and lenient movement. As a nurse herself, she found that yoga is a good combination of her job as a nurse and how they could help each other and make a unique class.

Her main goal is to bring happiness, confidence, mindfulness, and strength to all her students. While understanding the dynamics of movement and breath she will lead you through a gentle warm-up, a heat-building sequence, and finally, a relaxing cool down to recharge, release and relax you. “For me, yoga is about the balance between strength and flexibility, exertion and release. Expect to move, sweat, and breathe."
